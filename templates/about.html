About Us

We are Beat this Together, a University of Bristol society dedicated to raising awareness of eating disorders, increasing body positivity and body neutrality, and fundraising for Beat UK.

We've done this through a range of body positivity talks with sports societies, talks with schools about mental well-being at university, and various fundraising events within the University of Bristol. Beat this Together puts everything we can into battling eating disorder stigma and increasing awareness, and over the years has generated lots of attention, as well as participation, at the University.

If this sounds like your kind of thing and you fancy joining the team, go to the UoB SU website to join today!
